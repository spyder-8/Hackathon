Hey everyone. Long time lurker, but I had to post this because I know a lot of us obsessed over this back in the day.

Do you guys remember that incredibly ambitious indie RPG project from 2008? The one where the lead developer just randomly dropped off the face of the earth 6 months into development with zero explanation? The servers went down, the blog vanished, everything.

Well, I was just reading a local King County news aggregate, and... they found him. Or rather, they found his remains. His name was Elias Thorne.

But here is where it gets incredibly weird, and why I'm posting it on this board: Hikers found him in a subterranean bunker out in the woods. The door was welded shut from the inside. And according to the leaked info from the ME's office, there's no obvious cause of death. No trauma, no poison, nothing. Just weird "cellular degradation."

What the hell was he doing out there? Was it related to the game's lore?
