Search for Urban Explorer Called Off After 72 Hours

By Local Desk

Authorities have officially suspended the search for 22-year-old college student and amateur urban explorer, Chloe Price, who vanished in the deep woods of King County last Thursday.

Price's vehicle was found parked on an unpaved logging road. Inside, investigators discovered printouts of internet forums and a hand-drawn map leading to the decommissioned subterranean bunker where the remains of software engineer Elias Thorne were discovered earlier this year.

Search and rescue teams reported severe difficulties navigating the area where she vanished. K-9 units refused to enter a specific sector of the forest, and three different deputies reported their compasses spinning wildly when approaching the coordinates marked on Price's map.

Sheriff's department officials continue to urge the public to stay away from the restricted bunker zone due to "unstable terrain and environmental hazards."

Have Information?

If you have any information regarding the whereabouts of Chloe Price, please contact the King County Sheriff's Office immediately.
