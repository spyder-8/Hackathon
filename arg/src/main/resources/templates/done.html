THE WELDED HOUSE: It's Happening Again.

Everyone thought the bunker incident was an isolated event. A crazy dev who lost his mind. But it’s not isolated. It's a contagion.

David Chen was a freelance concept artist. He was the guy hired to draw the monsters for the game developed by Elias Thorne. Chen quit the project early on, claiming the descriptions he was given were "giving him night terrors."

Yesterday, police responded to a noise complaint at Chen's suburban home. When they arrived, they couldn't get inside. Why? Because every single door and window had been sealed. Not just locked. Welded shut. With thick sheets of metal.

It took the fire department three hours to cut through the front door. The house was empty. No Chen. But the walls... the walls were covered in charcoal drawings. It wasn't monsters this time. It was a single, repetitive landscape. A dead garden under a black sky.

He didn't weld the doors to keep people out. Like the developer, he welded them from the inside. He was trying to keep whatever was in there from getting out.
