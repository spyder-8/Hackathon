KING COUNTY SHERIFF'S OFFICE

OFFICIAL MISSING PERSONS BULLETIN

CASE #: 2018-11-209-F
DATE FILED: Nov 18, 2018

SUBJECT: VANCE, Eleanor

OCCUPATION: Head Archivist, Historical Society

NARRATIVE:

Subject was reported missing after failing to open the archives for three consecutive days. Subject had recently requested to pull municipal zoning records, historical maps, and folklore accounts from the late 1800s concerning the "Blackwood Anomaly."

Notes left on the subject's desk cross-referenced these historical anomalies with the digital maps extracted from the personal hard drives of Elias Thorne. Subject's notes suggested she believed Thorne was not designing a video game, but rather "mapping an overlap."

CCTV footage from the archive basement shows the subject walking into aisle 4 at 11:42 PM. She is never seen exiting the aisle. A physical search of aisle 4 revealed no hidden exits, but investigating officers noted a severe drop in temperature and the smell of ozone.

STATUS: OPEN / UNRESOLVED
