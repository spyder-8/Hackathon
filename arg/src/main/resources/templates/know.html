Computer Science Department Head Missing

Dr. Robert Lin fails to appear for lectures; office found in disarray.

The university administration has confirmed that Dr. Robert Lin, Head of the Computer Science Department, is currently missing. Classes have been cancelled indefinitely.

According to graduate assistants, Dr. Lin had recently taken a sabbatical to study legacy code from a discontinued indie game. Sources confirm the code was an early Alpha build given to him years ago by Elias Thorne, a former student of Dr. Lin who recently passed away under mysterious circumstances.

"He was running the code through a deep-learning analysis tool," said one TA who wished to remain anonymous. "He kept muttering that the architecture was impossible. That it wasn't rendering graphics, it was trying to render physical space."

Campus security breached Dr. Lin's locked office yesterday. They found the room completely empty of furniture, with complex, non-euclidean geometric patterns scratched deeply into the drywall. His computer was missing.
