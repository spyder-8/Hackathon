Twitch Streamer "GlitchHunter99" Disappears During Live Broadcast of Cursed RPG

The internet mystery community is in uproar today after popular lost-media streamer 'GlitchHunter99' seemingly vanished in the middle of a live broadcast to over 4,000 viewers.

The streamer had managed to acquire a playable build of the infamous, unreleased RPG created by the late Elias Thorne. Viewers reported that the game was running fine for the first hour, showcasing standard low-poly environments and cryptic dialogue.

However, at the 1:14:00 mark, the game's skybox shifted from blue to a pulsating, corrupted black. The streamer stopped talking. Over the webcam, viewers watched as the ambient lighting in the streamer's actual room began to match the corrupted skybox of the game.

The stream abruptly crashed. When local police conducted a wellness check four hours later, the apartment was empty. The PC was still running, but the monitor was cracked from the inside out, as if something had pushed its way through the glass.

Update: Twitch has permanently deleted the VOD of the incident, citing "safety concerns."
